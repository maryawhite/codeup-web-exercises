Denali National Park and Preserve FAQs

How many acres is Denali National Park?
Denali National Park and Preserve encompasses 6 million acres.
What is the highest peak in Denali?
The highest peak is Denali, aka Mt. McKinley, at 20,310 feet.
Do I have to stay on the designated trails?
No, in fact, visitors are encouraged to go off the trails.
Are there bears in Denali?
Yes, you will encounter all types of wildlife, including bears.
Are there bus tours available?
Yes, please check our website.
What about lodging?
Check out the Dome Home in nearby Healy.
Is there an entrance fee to get into the park?
Yes, $15 for a 7 day pass, $45 for an annual pass.
How far is it from Fairbanks to Denali?
121 miles/195 km. Roughly 2.5 hours by car.
How long does it take to drive from Atlanta to Alaska?
Four days with 2 drivers.
Are there mosquitos.
Yes, bring DEET
